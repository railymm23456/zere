Zere Amangeldinova


Zere Amangeldinova is a Kazakh-born photographer based in Kazakhstan.
Zere shoots candy-style shots where she attempts to capture life-like photographs with sunlight, man and nature in mind.
"I tell stories about the world we live in and the world we imagine. inside yourself, on the way to self-knowledge."
